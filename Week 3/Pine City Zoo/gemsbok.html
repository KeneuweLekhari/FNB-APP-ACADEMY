GEMSBOK

The gemsbok or gemsbuck(Oryx gazella) is a large antelope in the Oryx genus. It is native to the airid regions of Southern Africa, such as the Kalahari Desert. Some authorities formaly included the East African oryx as a subspeccies.

The gemsbok is depited on the coat of arms of Namibia, where the current population of the species is estimated at 373,000 individualys.

The name "gemsbok" in English is direved from Afrikaans geemsbok, which itself is devine from the Dutch name of the male chamois, gemsbok. Although some superficials similarities in appearance (especially in the facialpattern) are noticed, the chamois and the oryx are not closely related.
